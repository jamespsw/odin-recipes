Baião de Dois

Description

Baião de Dois is a traditional Brazilian dish from the Northeast region that combines rice and beans. It usually contains dried meat, sausage, and spices, making it a flavorful and hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 1 cup of black-eyed peas
  • 100g of dried meat
  • 100g of sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak the black-eyed peas overnight in water.
  2. The next day, cook the peas in boiling water until tender.
  3. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  4. Cut the dried meat and sausage into small pieces.
  5. In a large pan, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil until they are translucent.
  6. Add the dried meat and sausage to the pan and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add the cooked black-eyed peas and tomato to the pan and stir well.
  8. Finally, add the cooked rice to the pan and mix everything together.
  9. Season with salt to taste.
