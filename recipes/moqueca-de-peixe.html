Moqueca de Peixe

Description

Moqueca de Peixe is a flavorful Brazilian fish stew that is popular in the Northeast. It is typically made with white fish, onions, garlic, tomatoes, bell peppers, and coconut milk, which gives it a rich and creamy texture.

Ingredients

  • 500g of white fish fillets
  • 2 onions
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish into large pieces and season with salt.
  2. In a large pan, saut√© the onions and garlic in olive oil until they are soft.
  3. Add the tomatoes, bell peppers, and chili pepper to the pan and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add the fish to the pan and cook until it is almost done.
  5. Pour the coconut milk over the fish and vegetables and stir well.
  6. Simmer the stew for a few more minutes
  7. Adjust the seasoning with salt to taste.
  8. Serve the moqueca de peixe hot with rice and/or bread.
